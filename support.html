How to use Chirp

1. Tap the green microphone icon to start recording.
2. Tap the red stop icon to end the recording.
3. Your recordings are automatically saved and can be played back or deleted from the list.

Common Issues & Solutions

No sound during playback?
Make sure the app has microphone permissions enabled.

How do I delete a recording?
Tap the red trash can icon next to the recording you want to remove.
